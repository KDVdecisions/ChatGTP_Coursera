Product Description

\n

\n Introducing our latest addition to our mid-century inspired office furniture collection, the SWC-100 Chair. This chair is part of a beautiful family of furniture that includes filing cabinets, desks, bookcases, meeting tables, and more. With its sleek design and customizable options, it is suitable for both home and business settings.\n

\n

\n The SWC-100 Chair offers several options for customization. You can choose from a variety of shell colors and base finishes to match your desired aesthetic. The chair is available with plastic back and front upholstery or full upholstery in a range of fabric and leather options. The base finish options include stainless steel, matte black, gloss white, or chrome. You can also choose whether to have armrests or not, depending on your preference.\n

\n

\n The construction of the SWC-100 Chair ensures durability and functionality. It features a 5-wheel plastic coated aluminum base, providing stability and mobility. The chair also has a pneumatic adjuster, allowing for easy raise and lower action to find the perfect height for your comfort.\n

\n The SWC-100 Chair also offers additional options to suit your specific needs. You can choose between soft or hard-floor caster options for easy movement on different surfaces. The chair is available with two choices of seat foam densities: medium (1.8 lb/ft3) or high (2.8 lb/ft3). Additionally, you can opt for armless or 8 position PU armrests for added comfort and versatility.\n

\n

\n The materials used in the construction of the SWC-100 Chair ensure both quality and style. The shell and base glider are made of cast aluminum with a modified nylon PA6/PA66 coating, providing durability and a sleek appearance. The seat is made of HD36 foam, ensuring comfort during extended periods of use.\n

\n

\n The SWC-100 Chair is proudly made in Italy, known for its craftsmanship and attention to detail.\n
